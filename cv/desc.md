I am a member of the Rubin Observatory Legacy Survey of Space and Time (LSST) Dark Energy Science Collaboration (DESC). The DESC is an international collaboration of scientists working together to prepare for and analyze data from the LSST with the goal of understanding the nature of dark energy and its impact on the evolution of the Universe.

### Member of the following working groups:
- Point-To-Object (PO)
- Static Analysis Roundtable (STAR)
- Weak Lensing and Large Scale Structure (WLSS)
